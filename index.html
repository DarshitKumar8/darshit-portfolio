GEO / AI Search Visibility

Is Notion actually visible inside ChatGPT — or just assumed to be?

A structured 29-prompt audit measuring whether ChatGPT mentions, cites, and recommends Notion across brand, use-case, comparison, and buying-intent searches — and which competitors win the prompts Notion doesn't.

Problem

SaaS teams have no visibility into whether AI assistants recommend them when prospects ask discovery or buying questions — a growing blind spot as AI-mediated search scales.

Data

29 prompts across 4 intent categories (Brand, Use-Case, Comparison, Buying-Intent), tested directly against ChatGPT and logged for rank, citation, and competitor overlap.

Approach

Scored every response for mention, citation, and visibility, then aggregated into KPI dashboards to quantify performance by intent category, not anecdote.

Insight

55.17% mention rate, 37.93% citation rate, 48.28% top-3 visibility. Strong on brand search — but Microsoft OneNote, Obsidian, and ClickUp recur as the competitors AI surfaces instead on broader prompts.

Recommendation

Build head-to-head comparison content, expand use-case pages, and structure content (FAQs, comparison tables) so AI systems have something concrete to cite.

Technical SEO / Competitive Audit

Where does a category leader's SEO foundation actually have cracks?

A full-funnel technical, content, and competitive SEO audit of Zapier across 5 core pages and 5 direct competitors — turned into a prioritized 90-day roadmap, not a 40-item issue list.

Problem

Even category leaders need to know precisely where their SEO foundation has cracks before a fast-moving competitor exploits them.

Data

5 pages audited across 14+ technical SEO dimensions, plus a full backlink profile and keyword landscape benchmarked against Make, n8n, Power Automate, Workato, and IFTTT.

Approach

Structured technical audit, 32 keywords across 7 topical clusters, and competitive/keyword-gap analysis, synthesized into 12 dashboards and a sequenced roadmap.

Insight

Overall SEO Score 9.1/10. Technical foundation is excellent (DR 91, ~95K referring domains, ~10M backlinks) — the real gap is topical authority in enterprise and AI-automation content, where Microsoft and Workato are closing in.

Recommendation

90-day roadmap: Days 1–30 technical polish, Days 31–60 content expansion, Days 61–90 competitive content and authority building.

AI Platform Benchmarking

Does "optimizing for AI search" even mean the same thing across models?

A reproducible 20-prompt benchmark comparing ChatGPT and Gemini head-to-head on accuracy, completeness, actionability, and citation behavior — because a single-model GEO strategy is a blind spot most teams don't know they have.

Problem

Brands optimizing for "AI search" often optimize for one model's behavior without knowing whether it generalizes to others.

Data

20 identical prompts across 5 intent categories, run against both platforms in incognito mode (no follow-ups, no personalization bias) — 40 total AI responses scored.

Approach

Built a reproducible custom rubric (Accuracy, Completeness, Actionability) plus citation-rate tracking, automated for repeatable re-testing as models update.

Insight

Gemini beat ChatGPT overall (4.91 vs. 4.63) and cited sources far more often (85% vs. 10%), while both were equally accurate (5.0) and equally likely to recommend a product (55%).

Recommendation

Prioritize authoritative, well-structured content for Gemini-style retrieval while keeping key facts concisely extractable for ChatGPT — and re-run quarterly as models shift.
